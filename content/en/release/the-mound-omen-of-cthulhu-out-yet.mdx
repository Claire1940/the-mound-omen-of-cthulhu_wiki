The environment in this Lovecraftian setting is not just physically dangerous; it actively attacks your mental stability. As your team ventures deeper into the jungle, sanity levels drop, triggering a complex madness system. The curse manifests as visual and auditory hallucinations, making it difficult to distinguish real threats from illusions. The jungle will attempt to deceive you at every turn, altering the appearance of the terrain and disguising deadly traps as harmless mirages.

<Callout type="warning" title="Proximity Voice Chat Hazard">
Losing physical proximity to your teammates cuts off communication. A player who wanders off alone for too long risks being controlled by the jungle, turning them into a hostile threat to the remaining survivors.
</Callout>
