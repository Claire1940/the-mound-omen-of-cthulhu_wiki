<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat and the Shared Weapon System">

Combat in **the mound omen of cthulhu early access** is a desperate struggle for survival. Unlike many modern shooters, ammunition is extremely scarce, and the noise of a gunshot is often more dangerous than the enemies themselves. The game employs a shared weapon system that forces the party to act as a single unit.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Inventory Allocation">
    Before starting a run, the party must decide who carries the primary firearms and who focuses on melee defense. You cannot all be heavy hitters.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Resource Management">
    Ammunition found in the world is added to a shared pool. If one player spams shots, the entire team loses their ranged capability.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Noise Threshold">
    Every gunshot increases the "Jungle Alert" level. High alert levels cause more aggressive Lovecraftian entities to spawn and hunt the party.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Survival Strategies for the Cursed Jungle">

Surviving the jungle requires more than just good aim. You must manage your light sources, your sanity, and your physical health simultaneously. The environment is non-linear, meaning there are multiple paths to the underground city, each with its own risks.

**Key Survival Pillars:**
- **Light Management**: Darkness accelerates madness. Keep your torches lit, but remember they reveal your position to monsters.
- **Sanity Recovery**: Resting at specific "Sanctuaries" or using rare herbal consumables can lower your madness level.
- **Environmental Interaction**: Use the dense foliage to hide from larger threats. Not every encounter should be a fight.
- **Team Synergy**: Stay within visual range of your teammates. Being isolated in the jungle triggers a rapid increase in the madness meter.

| Consumable | Effect | Rarity |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Coca Leaves** | Small sanity boost, speed buff | Common |
| **Medicinal Herbs** | Heals physical wounds | Uncommon |
| **Blessed Oil** | Keeps torches burning longer | Rare |
| **Ancient Idol** | Resets madness for one player | Very Rare |

<Callout type="info" title="Important Note">
The game does not feature a traditional HUD. You must look at your character's hands (trembling) or listen to their breathing to gauge their current stress and health levels.
</Callout>
