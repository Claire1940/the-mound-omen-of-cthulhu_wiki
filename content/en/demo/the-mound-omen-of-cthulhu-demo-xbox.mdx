Surviving the cursed jungle requires more than just shooting skills. The game introduces mechanics that force players to cooperate closely while managing their sanity. The deeper your expedition pushes into the jungle, the more the environment reacts to your presence.

The madness mechanic is the most innovative aspect of the gameplay. As your sanity drops, your perception of reality warps. You will hear footsteps that do not exist, see shifting shadows, and lose the ability to distinguish your teammates from the monsters. This creates a real and functional friendly fire risk, leading to chaotic and stressful moments.

| Action | Danger Level | Environmental Impact |
| **Melee Attacks** | Low | Quiet execution, preserves team sanity |
| **Light Firearms** | Medium | Moderate noise, alerts nearby threats |
| **Heavy Firearms** | High | Extreme noise, escalates jungle aggression |
