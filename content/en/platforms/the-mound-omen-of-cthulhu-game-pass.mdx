<Section icon={Activity} title="Sanity and Perception Mechanics">

The most unique aspect of the mound omen of cthulhu is the **Perception System**. Unlike other horror games, there is no sanity meter. Instead, madness manifests as private visual and auditory hallucinations. This forces players to rely heavily on verbal communication to verify what is real.

**Common Hallucination Types:**
- **Bleeding Terrain**: The ground or trees appear to pulse with red veins or drip blood.
- **False Horns**: Hearing the ox cart's horn from a direction where the cart is not located.
- **Shadow Teammates**: Seeing a fifth explorer in the fog who mimics your team's movements.
- **Identity Distortion**: Teammates may briefly appear as monsters, leading to accidental friendly fire.
