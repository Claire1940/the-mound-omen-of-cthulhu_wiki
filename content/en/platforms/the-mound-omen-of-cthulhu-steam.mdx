<Section icon={Activity} title="Sanity and the Hallucination System">

One of the most innovative features of **the mound omen of cthulhu steam** is the lack of a traditional Sanity HUD. Instead of a bar that depletes, the game uses **sensory distortion** to communicate your mental state. As madness takes hold, the world around you changes in ways your teammates might not see.

**Common Hallucination Types:**
- **Visual Shifts**: The jungle may appear to bleed, or the moon may turn a deep, pulsing red.
- **Auditory Lures**: You might hear the extraction horn or a teammate calling for help when they are actually silent.
- **Identity Corruption**: Teammates may briefly appear as monsters, leading to tragic friendly fire incidents.
- **Environmental Traps**: False paths or "ghost" treasure that disappears upon contact.
