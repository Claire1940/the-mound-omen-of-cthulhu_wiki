<Section icon={Map} title="Beginner Expedition Walkthrough">

Survival in the cursed jungle requires a disciplined approach to the "extraction" loop. Every mission begins aboard the *Tempestad* galleon, where the crew selects a contract and prepares their loadout. Unlike traditional shooters, resources are limited and shared among the party, making the division of labor a critical first step.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Contract Selection">
    Visit the Captain on the galleon to choose an expedition. Contracts vary by objective, such as retrieving specific Tulu metal relics, rescuing survivors, or meeting a minimum treasure value.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Loadout Division">
    Assign roles based on equipment. One player should carry the oil lamp for visibility, another the bow for quiet kills, and a third should focus on the treasure-finding medallion.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Ox Cart Escort">
    The ox cart is your mobile hub. It stores your collected treasure and spare supplies. You must protect it from terrain blockages and monster attacks while moving toward the objective.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Extraction Decision">
    Once the contract is satisfied, the team must decide to push deeper for optional loot or retreat. Greed is a primary cause of squad wipes in the late-game jungle.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Resource Management">
Always deposit high-value relics into the ox cart immediately. If the squad is wiped, items carried in personal inventories are lost, but items in the cart may still be recoverable.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Sanity and Perception Systems">

The sanity system is the most unique mechanic in **the mound omen of cthulhu xbox**. It attacks the player's ability to communicate and trust their teammates. There is no sanity bar on the HUD; instead, you must rely on visual and auditory cues to determine if your character is losing their grip on reality.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Visual Hallucinations">
    - **Bleeding Walls**: The environment changes color.
    - **False Objects**: Loot items that disappear when touched.
    - **Shifting Paths**: Terrain that appears safe but leads to traps.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Auditory & Social Deception">
