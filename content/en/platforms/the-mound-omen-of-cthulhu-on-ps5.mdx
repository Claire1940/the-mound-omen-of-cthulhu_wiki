<Section icon={Shield} title="Surviving the Madness System">

The most unique aspect of this horror experience is its complex madness system. As you venture deeper into the jungle, the cosmic corruption takes a heavy toll on your character's mental state. Sanity depletion is not just a stat; it actively alters your perception of reality, forcing you to question everything you see and hear.

| Sanity State | Visual Effects | Auditory Effects | Gameplay Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Stable | Clear, natural colors | Standard environmental audio | Normal movement and accurate mapping |
| Paranoid | Desaturated colors, shadows flicker | Faint whispers, distant footsteps | False trap markers appear on the ground |
| Insane | Blood-drenched screen, heavy distortion | Muffled voices, deceptive directional audio | High risk of friendly fire and teammate possession |

When sanity drops to critical levels, the jungle begins to play tricks on your communication. Spatialized voice chat can become distorted, making distant friends sound right next to you, or vice versa. If a teammate wanders too far into the darkness while insane, they may lose control entirely, returning to the group as a hostile entity controlled by the jungle.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Progression and Fort Unlocks">

The cursed jungle is divided into large, open zones connected by old, abandoned forts built by previous, failed expeditions. Exploring these structures is critical for long-term progression. By locating and recovering logbooks hidden within these ruins, you permanently unlock the forts as starting points for future runs.

| Fort Name | Region | Difficulty | Primary Reward |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Fort San Diego | Coastal Swamps | Low | Swamp Starting Point, Basic Supplies |
| Fort Jaguar | Deep Jungle | Medium | Jungle Starting Point, Advanced Muskets |
| Fort Cthulhu | The Mound Entrance | High | Direct Access to The Mound, Rare Relics |

Unlocking these forts allows you to bypass the outer regions of the map, saving valuable time and keeping your sanity intact for the final push into The Mound itself. However, starting deeper in the jungle means facing tougher monsters right from the beginning of your run.

<Callout type="tip" title="Progression Strategy">
Focus your first few expeditions entirely on locating Fort San Diego's logbook. Having a secure starting point closer to the inner jungle makes gathering high-tier treasures much safer.
</Callout>
