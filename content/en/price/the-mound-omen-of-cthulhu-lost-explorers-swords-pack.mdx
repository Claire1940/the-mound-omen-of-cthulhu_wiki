<Section icon={Map} title="Sanity and Hallucination Mechanics">

The jungle does more than just hide physical monsters; it actively attacks the minds of the explorers. There is no visible sanity bar in the HUD. Instead, players must rely on their own senses and squad communication to determine what is real.

**Common Hallucinations to Watch For:**
- **False Teammates**: Seeing a silhouette of a friend in the fog who is actually across the map.
- **Bleeding Terrain**: The ground or trees may appear to pulse with red light or drip blood.
- **Phantom Sounds**: Hearing the extraction horn or a teammate's voice when no one has spoken.
- **Transformed Allies**: A teammate might briefly appear as a monster, leading to accidental friendly fire.
