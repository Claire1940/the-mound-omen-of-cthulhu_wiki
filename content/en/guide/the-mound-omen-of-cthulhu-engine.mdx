<Section icon={Shield} title="Survival Strategies for the Jungle">

Surviving the **the mound omen of cthulhu engine** requires more than just good aim. The environment itself is an antagonist. Use these strategies to increase your chances of reaching the Mound and returning with your sanity intact.

- **Maintain Line of Sight**: In the dense jungle, it is easy to lose track of teammates. If the proximity chat cuts out, use your oil lamp to signal your position.
- **Trigger Discipline**: Do not fire at every shadow. Use the crossbow for scouts and save the musket for the unspeakable Lovecraftian creatures that require heavy stopping power.
- **Logbook Priority**: On your first few runs, ignore the treasure. Focus entirely on finding forts and recovering logbooks to build a network of safe zones.
- **Sound Awareness**: Listen for the sound of cracking branches. The engine uses specialized sound to give you a split-second warning before an ambush.
