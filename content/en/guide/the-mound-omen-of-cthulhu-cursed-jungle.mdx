The environment does not just attack your physical health; it wages war on your mind. The deep jungle is governed by a dynamic sanity system that triggers illusions and hallucinations as your mental state deteriorates. If your sanity drops too low, the line between reality and hallucination blurs completely.

Proximity voice chat plays a vital role in managing your sanity. If a player wanders too far from the group, they will lose radio contact. During this isolation, the jungle can manipulate their senses or even possess them. When a lost teammate returns, you must verify their identity to ensure they are not a hostile doppelganger controlled by the curse.

| Sanity Level | Visual Effects | Audio Effects | Hazard Risk |
| 75% - 100% | Clear vision | Normal ambient sounds | None |
| 50% - 74% | Minor color shifts | Faint whispers | Low |
| 25% - 49% | Blood-drenched screen | Loud hallucinations | Medium |
| 0% - 24% | Mirage traps active | Total silence | **High (Possession)** |

<Callout type="info" title="Proximity Chat Tip">
If a teammate stops responding to proximity voice chat, do not approach them alone. Regroup with your remaining allies and approach with weapons drawn to counter potential possession.
