<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The Tempest Dot: Your Base of Operations Lore">

Between the harrowing descents, players return to the *Tempest Dot*, a galleon that serves as a floating hub. This ship is rife with a "noxious atmosphere," yet it is the only place of relative safety. The NPCs on board provide the necessary flavor to the world's grim setting.

- **The Captain**: A demanding figure who provides the contracts that drive the story forward.
- **The Cook**: Advises on the best foodstuffs to retrieve from the land to maintain stamina.
- **The Lute Player**: Offers a brief respite from the silence of the jungle with period-accurate tunes.
- **The Ship's Dog**: A small comfort in a world of cosmic madness.

The ship's deck is where strategy happens. Since inventory is limited to six slots, players must use the oxcart—which trails the expedition—to store surplus loot. This logistics management reflects the "expeditionary" nature of the lore, where every item brought back is a victory against the encroaching darkness.

| Hub Feature | Function | Lore Significance |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Contract Table** | Mission Start | The Captain's greed drives the plot |
| **Oxcart Trunk** | Storage | Represents the team's shared burden |
| **Cook's Galley** | Food Advice | Survival depends on local resources |
| **Resident Lute** | Immersion | The last remnants of human culture |

<Callout type="tip" title="Hub Interaction">
Always talk to the Cook before a run; knowing which food items are most valuable can save your inventory space during a scavenge.
