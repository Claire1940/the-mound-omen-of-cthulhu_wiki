<Section icon={Shield} title="Madness and Sanity Management">

As you venture deeper into the jungle, your character's sanity will begin to decay. Madness is not just a visual effect; it actively warps your perception of reality, creating illusions that can lead to your demise. You will experience sensory distortions, muffled audio, and terrifying hallucinations.

Hallucinations can manifest as fake loot traps, spectral enemies disguised as allies, or your actual teammates appearing as monsters. To mitigate these effects, you must employ specific sanity management strategies. Staying close to your team and remaining near the oxcart are the most effective ways to keep madness at bay.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Best Weapons and Combat Tactics">

Combat in this game requires precision and resource conservation. Since ammunition is scarce and firearms are unreliable in wet weather, mastering melee combat is crucial. Different weapons offer unique advantages, and understanding their utility can mean the difference between life and death.

The throwing axe stands out as one of the best weapons in the game due to its infinite reuse potential. Simply throw it at an enemy and retrieve it from their corpse. For defensive play, the crucifix can slow down enemy aggression and draw attention, allowing your teammates to slip behind monsters for easy backstabs.

  <Step num="2" title="Jungle Scavenging">
