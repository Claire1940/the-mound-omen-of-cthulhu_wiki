<Section icon={Shield} title="The Mound Omen of Cthulhu Armor and Protection Mechanics">

In *The Mound: Omen of Cthulhu*, conventional modern armor does not exist. Survival in this 16th-century Lovecraftian expedition depends on historical explorer garments, tactical loadouts, and sanity management. Your physical defense is heavily dictated by the weight of your gear, your choice of melee backup weapons, and how well you mitigate environmental hazards like mud, rain, and thick fog.

Rather than chasing stat-heavy chestplates, explorers must focus on utility. Carrying heavy firearms like the Matchlock Musket slows down your movement, making you a easier target for reanimated guardians. Conversely, running light with a Bow and a Knife increases your evasion and keeps your noise profile low.

| Protection Type | Primary Benefit | Main Drawback | Best Situation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Light Explorer Garb** | High movement speed, quiet | Minimal physical defense | Stealth scouting, rainy weather |
| **Standard Expedition Gear** | Balanced protection, decent slots | Moderate noise generation | General jungle exploration |
| **Deluxe Abyssal Set** | Premium cosmetic theme | Visual only, no stat boost | Group customization |

<Callout type="warning" title="Weight Matters">
Heavy loadouts drain your stamina faster. If you are cornered by a pack of Undead Wanderers, low stamina will prevent you from escaping or climbing to safety.
</Callout>

The weather in the cursed jungle is highly dynamic. Rain dampens match cords and ruins loose gunpowder, rendering your muskets useless. Always ensure at least two team members carry rain-proof alternatives.

| Weapon / Tool | Damage Type | Noise Level | Rain Usability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Bow** | Silent Ranged | Very Low | Excellent (No powder needed) |
| **Flintlock Musket** | Heavy Ranged | Extremely High | Poor (Misfires in downpours) |
| **Knife** | Fast Melee | Silent | Perfect (No ammo required) |
| **Matchlock Musket** | Sustained Ranged | High | Unusable (Match goes out) |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">
Never fire a musket unless it is a absolute emergency. The sound ripples through the jungle, waking up nearby Y'm-bhi Sentries and spawning additional hordes.
</Callout>
