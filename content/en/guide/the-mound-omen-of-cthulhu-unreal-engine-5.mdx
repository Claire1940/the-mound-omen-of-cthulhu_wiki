<Section icon={Map} title="Navigating the Cursed Jungle">

The jungle is not a linear path; it is a series of large open areas connected by abandoned forts. These forts are critical for long-term progression. By recovering logbooks left by previous expeditions, you can unlock these locations as starting points for future runs, allowing your team to venture deeper into the unknown without retracing every step.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Landing on the Mainland">
    Take the rowboat from the Galleon to the shore. Ensure all players have their assigned gear ready before stepping off the boat.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Coordinated Exploration">
    Use **Proximity Voice Chat** to stay in touch. If you wander too far, you lose communication, which is the greatest danger in the jungle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Fort Securing">
    Locate abandoned forts and search for logbooks. Securing these records ensures that your progress is saved for the next expedition.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Return Trip">
    Once the Captain's expectations are met (treasure or food), navigate back to the shore. The jungle becomes more hostile as night falls.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Exploration Phase | Objective | Risk Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Shoreline** | Initial looting and scouting | **Low** |
| **Deep Jungle** | Finding treasure and logbooks | **High** |
| **The Mound** | Entering the Cthulhu-cursed ruins | **Critical** |
| **Extraction** | Getting back to the rowboat | **Variable** |

<Callout type="warning" title="Sound Traps">
The jungle breeds horrors that react to sound. Avoid cracking branches or firing muskets unless absolutely necessary. Stealth is your best weapon.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Brain} title="Madness and the Sanity System">

In **The Mound Omen of Cthulhu Unreal Engine 5**, your greatest enemy isn't always a physical monster—it's your own mind. The madness system is a complex mechanic that triggers based on your proximity to Lovecraftian artifacts and the duration of your stay in the jungle. As your sanity drops, the world begins to deceive you.

| Sanity Level | Visual/Audio Effects | Gameplay Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Stable** | Clear vision, normal audio | Standard gameplay |
| **Shaken** | Minor whispers, edge blurring | Slight disorientation |
| **Delusional** | Blood-drenched world, mirages | Traps may look like treasure |
| **Insane** | Severe hallucinations, loss of control | Friends may appear as monsters |
