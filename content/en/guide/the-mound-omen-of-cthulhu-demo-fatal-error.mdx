<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Resolution">

If you are stuck at the launch screen, follow these logical steps to restore functionality. Most users report success by addressing display settings first.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Check Monitor Configuration">
    If you are using a laptop with an external monitor, ensure the laptop screen is active when launching the game. Some users find that closing the laptop lid before the game fully initializes causes a fatal error.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Verify Primary Display Settings">
    Go to your Windows display settings and ensure that the monitor you intend to play on is set as your "Main Display."
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Update Graphics Drivers">
    Ensure your NVIDIA or AMD drivers are updated to the latest version. Older drivers may not correctly handle the DirectX 12 calls required by the game.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Clear Local Cache">
    Navigate to `%localappdata%\TheMound\Saved\` and delete the contents of the 'Crashes' or 'Config' folders to reset user-defined graphic settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">
If the error persists, try setting your GPU display mode to "Auto" in your laptop's control software (e.g., ASUS Armoury Crate) instead of forcing "Discrete" or "Integrated" modes.
</Callout>

</Section>
