<Section icon={Shield} title="Key Enemy Mechanics and Grab Attacks">

The most frustrating aspect of combat that players encounter is the relentless grab attacks. Hostile creatures are designed to actively prevent players from endlessly kiting them. Undead monsters run faster than human explorers, making escape on foot virtually impossible once you have been spotted. If you hear heavy breathing while running, it means a creature is directly behind you and about to initiate a grab.

These grab attacks are not just basic physical strikes; they can force you to drop your active weapon, leaving you completely defenseless in the middle of a chaotic skirmish. 

| Grab Variant | Enemy Source | Immediate Effect | Best Countermeasure |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Lunging Grab | Fast-running Undead | Temporarily pins player, inflicts physical damage | Turn and strike with melee, do not run |
| Tendril Disarm | Tentacle Blob | Forces player to drop active weapon | Use backstab attacks while teammate distracts |
| Subterranean Grab | Underground Undead | Drags player down, inflicts heavy physical damage | Stay close to bots or human teammates |
| Teleportation Grab | Tree Guy | Teleports player into nearby rivers or hazards | Avoid fighting near log bridges or cliffs |

The game features an aggressive isolation mechanic. If you scout ahead by yourself, separate from your human allies, or let your companion bot wander off, the game severely punishes you. Both the underground grab attacks and the devastating health-draining tentacle attacks are triggered primarily when a player is isolated. 

<Callout type="tip" title="Stay Together">
Never wander off alone to search for treasure. Staying within physical proximity of your team or bot companion disables the automatic underground grab triggers, keeping your squad safe.
</Callout>
