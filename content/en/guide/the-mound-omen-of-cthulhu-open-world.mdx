<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Tactics and Stealth">

Direct combat in the jungle is highly dangerous and should be treated as a last resort. The environment reacts to the noise you make. Firing a musket or even stepping on dry branches can awaken the surrounding territory, drawing aggressive Lovecraftian creatures to your location. Stealth and coordination are your best tools for survival.

When combat is unavoidable, use silent weapons like the crossbow or sword to dispatch isolated threats. If a player must fire a musket, the rest of the team should immediately prepare for a counter-attack or plan a quick retreat route.

| Noise Source | Detection Range | Threat Level | Recommended Reaction |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Cracking Branch** | Short range | Low | Freeze and crouch |
| **Crossbow Fire** | Medium range | Low | Reposition immediately |
| **Musket Shot** | Screen-wide | Extreme | Retreat to high ground |
| **Sprinting** | Medium range | Medium | Walk to break line of sight |

<Section icon={Shield} title="The Madness System and Proximity Chat">

The deeper you venture into the cursed territory, the heavier the toll on your sanity. The game features a dynamic madness system that triggers visual and auditory hallucinations. As your sanity drops, the environment distorts, traps masquerade as safe pathways, and the world may appear drenched in blood.

Communication is also affected by your sanity levels. The game utilizes specialized proximity voice chat, which becomes distorted or completely disabled as madness takes hold. If a teammate wanders too far and loses communication, they run the risk of being controlled by the jungle, turning them into a hostile threat to the rest of the group.

| Sanity Level | Visual Effects | Auditory Effects | Gameplay Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **High (80-100%)** | Normal vision | Clear audio | No penalties |
| **Medium (40-79%)** | Minor color shifts | Distant whispers | Traps look like items |
| **Low (1-39%)** | Blood-red screen | Severe static | Proximity chat disabled |
| **Zero (0%)** | Total hallucination | Constant screaming | Jungle takes control |
