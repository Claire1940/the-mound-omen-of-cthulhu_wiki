<Section icon={Shield} title="Step-by-Step Navigation and Cart Escort Strategy">

The core loop of every expedition revolves around protecting your ox cart as it travels across the map. The cart acts as your mobile base, storing your collected treasure, spare food, and weapons. If the cart is destroyed or lost, the expedition ends in failure.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Plot the Route on the Galleon">
    Before leaving the ship, review the captain's contract. Trace a path from the shoreline to the nearest fort or ruin, ensuring the path is wide enough for the ox cart.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Clear Obstacles Ahead">
    Send a scout ahead with a quiet weapon like a bow. Clear fallen trees, thick brush, and roaming undead before moving the cart forward.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Establish Defensive Perimeters">
    When searching ruins, park the cart in a defensible clearing. Assign at least one player to guard the cart while the others gather resources.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Deposit Valuables Immediately">
    Do not hoard treasure in your personal inventory. Regularly transfer relics and Tulu metal to the cart to keep your weight low and preserve stamina.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Signal the Retreat">
    Once the contract goal is met, sound the cart's horn to begin the extraction back to the shore. Make sure all players are grouped together before moving.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Map Distortions and Sanity Effects">

The map in The Mound: Omen of Cthulhu is heavily influenced by your squad's mental state. There is no sanity bar on your screen; instead, the physical environment changes as madness takes hold. Landmarks will shift, paths will appear to block off, and the colors of the jungle will warp into terrifying hues.

| Sanity Level | Visual Map Changes | Auditory Hallucinations | Navigation Impact |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Stable** | Clear visibility, natural green foliage | Normal jungle sounds, wildlife | Reliable compass, clear landmarks |
| **Unstable** | Localized blood rain, pulsing red plants | Distant whispering, false cart horns | Paths seem to shift, fake terrain blocks |
| **Fractured** | Thick red fog, bleeding stone walls | Screams, fake teammate callouts | Compass spins wildly, landmarks disappear |

<Callout type="warning" title="Illusionary Paths">
When sanity is fractured, players may see completely different versions of the map. One player might see a clear path, while another sees a solid stone wall. Always verify routes using spatial voice chat.
</Callout>
