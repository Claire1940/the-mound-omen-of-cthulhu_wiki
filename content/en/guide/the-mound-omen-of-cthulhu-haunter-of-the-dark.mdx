<Section icon={Target} title="Walkthrough: The Haunter of the Dark Questline">

The "Haunter of the Dark" is the pivotal mid-game questline that takes you deep into the heart of the Mound. Following these steps carefully is required to avoid the "Eternal Darkness" ending.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Starry Wisdom Church">
    Locate the ruins of the church in the northern sector of the map. You must find the **Shining Trapezohedron** hidden in the steeple. Warning: Touching the artifact without gloves causes immediate Sanity damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Deciphering the Mound Scripts">
    Take the artifact to the Old Scholar in the Mound hub. You will need to protect him from a wave of Cultists while he translates the ancient texts required to open the Black Gate.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Entering the Abyss">
    Use the translated incantation at the Black Gate during a New Moon (check the in-game calendar). This leads to the "Haunter's Realm," a procedurally generated dungeon where light sources deplete 50% faster.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Final Confrontation">
    Face the Haunter of the Dark. You cannot kill it with traditional weapons. You must align the three **Primal Mirrors** to focus sunlight into the pit, banishing the entity back to the void.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Expert Strategy">
During Step 3, don't try to explore every corner. The Haunter's Realm is designed to trap players in infinite loops. Follow the "Blue Moss" trail to find the exit quickly.
</Callout>

</Section>
