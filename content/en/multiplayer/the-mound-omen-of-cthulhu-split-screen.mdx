<Section icon={Shield} title="The Mound Omen of Cthulhu Split Screen and Local Play Status">

Many players diving into ACE Team's Lovecraftian expedition horror game want to know if they can play with a friend on the same couch. Unfortunately, **the mound omen of cthulhu split screen** mode is not supported. The game is designed from the ground up as an online-only cooperative extraction experience. 

Because the game renders dense jungle environments, volumetric fog, dynamic lighting, and complex sanity-warping illusions for each explorer individually, a local split-screen mode would place too much strain on hardware resources. To play together, each explorer must use their own device and connect via online matchmaking or private lobbies.

<Section icon={Map} title="Online Co-op and Crossplay Features">

While local multiplayer is absent, the online cooperative features are highly robust. The game supports cross-platform play, allowing PC, PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series X|S players to form unified expedition parties. Xbox players also benefit from **Xbox Play Anywhere**, enabling seamless cross-progression and co-op between Windows PC and Xbox Series X|S consoles.

To coordinate effectively, the game features **spatialized voice chat**. This means your teammates' voices fade, echo, or change direction based on their actual location in the cursed jungle. If an explorer wanders too far into the fog, their voice will become muffled and difficult to track, heightening the tension.

| Co-op Feature | Supported Specifications | Practical Gameplay Impact |

<Callout type="tip" title="Communication is Key">
Do not rely on external party chat apps if you want the intended horror experience. The spatialized voice chat is directly tied to the sanity system, and losing voice contact with your squad is a core mechanic of the game's psychological terror.

<Section icon={Swords} title="How to Setup Online Cooperative Expeditions">

Starting a multiplayer expedition requires preparing your party aboard the *Tempestad* galleon before launching your excursion into the cursed jungle. Follow these steps to get your team ready.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Form Your Lobby">
    Invite your friends to your session using your platform's overlay or the in-game crossplay social menu. Confirm all players are visible on deck before proceeding.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select a Captain's Contract">
    Interact with the Captain's table to select your expedition contract. Agree on the required treasure value and the exit conditions before accepting the job.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Distribute the Shared Gear">
    The Captain provides shared tools rather than individual loadouts. Allocate weapons, the oil lamp, and treasure-finding tools among your party members.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Deploy to the Jungle">
    Once roles are assigned, head to the landing boat to begin the descent. Stick close to the ox cart as soon as you touch down on the shoreline.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Loadout Balance Tip">
Never put all your essential tools on a single player. If the explorer carrying the only **Oil Lamp** or the **Treasure-Finding Medallion** is incapacitated, the remaining team will be left in complete darkness and unable to track objectives.
</Callout>
