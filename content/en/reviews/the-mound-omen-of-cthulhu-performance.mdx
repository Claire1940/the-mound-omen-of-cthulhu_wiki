<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Optimization Guide">

Follow these steps to ensure your PC is fully optimized for the eldritch horrors awaiting you in The Mound.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Update Graphics Drivers">
    Download the latest Game Ready drivers from NVIDIA or AMD. These drivers often contain specific optimizations for the Omen of Cthulhu engine that can resolve day-one flickering.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Configure Windows Power Plan">
    Set your Windows Power Plan to "High Performance" and ensure "Game Mode" is toggled ON in the system settings to prioritize CPU cycles for the game.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="In-Game Scaling Setup">
    Navigate to Video Settings and enable DLSS/FSR. For 4K displays, the "Quality" or "Balanced" preset offers the best results. Turn on "Reflex Low Latency" if available.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Fine-Tune Shadows and Fog">
    Lower "Volumetric Fog" to Medium. This setting simulates the dense mist of the Mound but is extremely taxing on the GPU's compute units.
  </Step>
</Steps>
