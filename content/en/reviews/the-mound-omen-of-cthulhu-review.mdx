<Section icon={Shield} title="The Core Loop: Contracts, Treasure, and Extraction">

Every expedition begins aboard your ship, where you select a contract. These agreements dictate your required treasure value, specific relics to locate, or survivors to rescue. Once on land, your team must escort a slow-moving ox cart. This cart serves as your mobile vault, supply crate, and safety net.

| Expedition Phase | Primary Objective | Key Risk Factor |
|---|---|---|
| **1. Preparation** | Choose a contract and distribute limited tools on the galleon. | Poor distribution leaves players without backup weapons. |
| **2. Infiltration** | Escort the ox cart through shorelines and dense foliage. | Loud movements wake the jungle and attract undead sentries. |
| **3. Collection** | Search ruins for Tulu metal, relics, and survival food. | Treasure-detecting medallions make loud clangs when struck. |
| **4. Extraction** | Return the loaded ox cart to the galleon safely. | Greed encourages deeper exploration as resources dwindle. |

The extraction loop is a constant battle of risk versus reward. Pushing deeper into the jungle yields higher-value relics, but it exposes your crew to harsher weather, depleting ammunition, and escalating mental decay.

<Callout type="warning" title="Greed Kills">
If your squad wipes, you lose your collected haul. It is always safer to extract early with a completed contract than to chase optional relics with a fractured, low-ammo squad.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat and Gear: Period Weapons and Weather Dynamics">

Combat in *The Mound* is slow, heavy, and highly tactical. Flintlock and matchlock muskets pack immense stopping power but suffer from agonizingly slow reload times. Furthermore, gunpowder is highly susceptible to wet weather, rendering firearms completely useless during heavy downpours.

| Weapon Type | Combat Role | Major Strength | Critical Weakness |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Bow** | Stealth / Consistent DPS | Quiet; unaffected by rain; fast reload | Lower immediate damage than firearms |
| **Flintlock Musket** | High-Impact Defense | Extreme damage to physical targets | Loud; slow reload; fails in wet weather |
| **Knife** | Silent Melee Backup | Silent; requires no ammunition; rain-proof | Requires entering dangerous melee range |
| **Matchlock Musket** | Heavy Firepower | High damage against physical monsters | Extremely slow reload; useless in rain |

Because of these limitations, a balanced team loadout is mandatory. If every player carries a musket, a sudden rainstorm will leave the entire squad defenseless.

  <Step num="1" title="Equip the Scout">
